about me

Born curious, I never stop exploring, whether it be places, hobbies, or student organizations. Everything interests me, and everything I do is guided by my strong moral compass: people first. My relationships with my friends and family are and always will be my biggest priority.

I am a student at University of California, Berkeley, studying Computer Science and Statistics. Why both? I love to solve puzzles and always had a love for numbers, and my indecisiveness and ambition got the best of me. My current academic interests are machine learning and computer vision. My goal is to use my knowledge in these two fields to work on self-driving cars.

People always say that I have too many hobbies, but the three I'm working on now are travel, music, and photography. Look below for more info!
